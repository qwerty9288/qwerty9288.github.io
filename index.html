Who am I

I am a 14 year old student in Sweden. In the eighth grade we get to choose where we wan’t to do our prao. Prao is a thing that lets us choose where we want to work for two weeks, to accumulate work and real life experience. I chose to prao with my dad at EMQ Sweden AB, a company developing EMQX.

Goals of my project

So what were the goals of my prao? The biggest goal for all praos, as previously mentioned, is to accumulate work experience. Goals that are specific to what I have done in these two weeks include: get a general understanding of how it feels to be a software engineer, improve my programming skills, mostly in python, and to do something useful (which is also a common goal for praoing students.
